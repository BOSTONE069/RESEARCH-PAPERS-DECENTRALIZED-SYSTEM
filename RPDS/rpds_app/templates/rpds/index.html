RESEARCH PAPERS DECENTRALIZED SYSTEM(RPDS)

Welcome to our decentralized research paper publication platform built on the Ethereum blockchain! Our platform provides a secure and transparent way for researchers to publish their work and receive recognition for their contributions to the field. With our decentralized system, research papers are stored on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring that they cannot be tampered with or lost. Researchers can easily upload their papers and receive feedback from their peers through our peer review system. Additionally, our platform offers a rewards system for peer reviewers and authors, incentivizing participation and collaboration. Join our community of researchers and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in a decentralized and transparent manner
